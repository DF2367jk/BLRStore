Retirement planning

Regardless of if you have a traditional defined contribution or defined distribution retirement plan. You may have a 401k, IRA, 529, etc. You need other options. Start early to take advantage of compounding interest.

IUL or VUL

One option is an indexed or variable life insurance policy. We can set up a life policy that purchases a minimal amount of insurance then saves money using the insurance companies’ ability to grow money on a pretax or qualified account. This is the only way to grow your money tax free and to have access to your money without fees and penalties if you need it. You have life insurance until you pass. You pay or save over a fixed number of years. The Cash Value protects you prior to retirement then can pay you during your retirement while maintaining life insurance.

Non correlated funds during market downturns

This is even more important when you look at a chart of how the US stock market performs. We should be negative every 7 years. If you are living off your retirement plan, your portfolio, and other savings, the last thing you want to do is spend your principal! When we are in a down stock market year, replace the interest you would have taken from your portfolio with money from your life insurance and preserve your principal!
