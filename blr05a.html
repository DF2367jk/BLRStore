You Have Choices

You are young. You have choices. Since you are young the choices are more affordable.

Look at the periods of your life and imagine what your family will need at each step (a little or a lot)

Single (need to protect your income and have access to money in case you get hurt)

Married (number 1 plus protecting your income for at least 10 years)

Homeowner (protect the mortgage also)

Children (now it is time to speak with your spouse re feelings related to education)

Children (saving a little each week from day one can have a more profound effect than education)

Children have completed their education and are on their own (do you need as much)

Mortgage paid for (do you need as much)

Retired (life insurance may provide you with monthly checks)

Health issues but going to survive (life may provide you with a very large check)

You pass, did you choose to provide a lump sum or a fixed monthly amount for a number of years?
