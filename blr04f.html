How can you pass suddenly and still be a hero?

This is my little brother's story. The three little girls !

One evening my sister-in-law was startled by a loud thump upstairs. She investigated and found my little brother on the floor in pain just before he lost consciousness. . Although he was my little brother by age, he was a very strong, tough, weightlifter who was indestructible.

He had tripped over one of the dogs, his own feet, something... He caught a corner of a piece of furniture under his ribs. His wife was a nurse and started CPR while requesting an ambulance on speaker phone. It took the ambulance 6 minutes to arrive. He had damaged an organ that started to bleed internally. He passed before the paramedics could take over the CPR.

The hero and the three little girls

That is correct, young family, mortgage, two car loans, payments on one of the dog's recent surgeries! AND... three little girls. The youngest just started school!

He had a $500,000 term life insurance policy which was in effect. It took a little paperwork and 30 days for the insurance company to write a large check. They did send a smaller amount upon notice he had passed.

They were able to remain in their home, finish school with their friends, some are married, one made me a grand uncle, one is still in school.

This story would have ended much differently if he had not maintained his term life policy. I know he had it for a while as he was diagnosed with cancer 5 years earlier and became ineligible for more life insurance at that point. The cancer was in remission when he passed.
