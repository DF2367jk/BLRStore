Know someone who became disabled full or partial after an accident?

The brother of one of my friends was in a terrible car accident. He never walked again. However, he was able to return to work in a powerchair after a great deal of therapy.

His home had to be modified. He needed to purchase a modified van that was accessible. He could not drive. He needed a live-in care giver. His life expectancy was greatly affected and he eventually passed.

In addition to the auto insurance settlement from the accident, the disability insurance from his employer, he needed every penny he could find. His ability to earn was reduced and his expenses increased.

His requirement for assistance would have allowed him to use part of the death benefit from his life insurance if it was set up to allow this option.
